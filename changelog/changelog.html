To create your own bangs, click on the extension and you'll be taken to the options page with your custom bangs (initially empty).

In order to add a new custom bang, click on + Add Bang. Then input:

  • Name: How you want to call the bang. This is just for you to quickly identify bangs.
  • URL: The URL that the bang targets. Don't forget to specify the part that should be replace with {{{s}}}.
  • Bang: The bang (shortcut) itself. If a bang is already defined by DuckDuckGo, this one will have preference.
  • URL Encode Query: Whether the query (i.e., what comes after the bang) must be encoded or not. It is recommended to only turn it on when a bang doesn't work well without this setting.

Furthermore, you can back up or restore your bangs at any point. For that, scroll down and click on Backup / Restore.
